Here's a time line of Dr. Wangari Maathi's life:

  • 1940-Nyeri, Kenya
  • 1964-Obtained a degree in Biological Sciences from Mount St. Scholastica College in Atchison, Kansas
  • 1966-Earned a Master of Science degree from the University of Pittsburgh.
  • 1969-Gets married to Mwangi Mathai.
  • 1971-Obtained a Ph.D. from the University of Nairobi.
  • 1977-She establishes the Green Belt Movement (GBM) which an environmental organization that empowers communities, particularly women, to conserve the environment and improve livelihoods
  • 1979-Gets divorced to her husband,who the had 3 children together.
  • 1982-Tried contesting for the vacant Nyeri parliament seat but was disqualified on technicality by the court.
  • 1989-Opposes the construction of a 60 storey building in Uhuru Park.
  • 1991-she received the Goldman Environmental Prize in San Francisco and the Hunger Project's Africa Prize for Leadership in London.
  • 1997-Vied for the presidential seat on Lieral Party of Kenya but lost to the incubent president Moi.
  • 2003-she was appointed Assistant Minister in the Ministry for Environment and Natural Resources and served in that capacity until November 2005.
  • 2004 - was awarded the 2004 Nobel Peace Prize for her "contribution to sustainable development, democracy and peace.
  • 2011 - Died of complications arising from ovarian cancer while receiving treatment at a Nairobi hospital
